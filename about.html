About the Team

The Upper Arlington Men's Swim Team is one of the most competitive and accomplished high school swim teams in the state of Ohio. With decades of history, countless state titles, and athletes who have gone on to swim at collegiate and national levels, this program is built on a tradition of excellence.

Our team includes a diverse group of athletes who are dedicated to performance, sportsmanship, and representing Upper Arlington with pride. Our coaching staff is committed to developing swimmers both in and out of the pool, emphasizing hard work, discipline, and team unity.

Whether you're a parent, student, prospective athlete, or recruiter — this site is your go-to hub for all things Golden Bear Swimming.
